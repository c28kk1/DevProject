Striker

What should you be doing as a striker?

The job of a striker is pretty simple: put the ball in the back of the net. As a striker you should always be looking to get a shot off. 46% of goals have a random influence such as a deflection, so the more shots you take the more goals you will score. Remember, not all goals have to be pretty; a tap-in is worth the same amount of points as a shot.
